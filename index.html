News and Announcements


Saturday 6th July 2019

FuZioN wins the ASCII Votesheet for Versus #9 competition at the Edison 2019 demoparty

We're really proud to announce that FuZioN has entered an ASCII Votesheet competition.. and won it!

The competition was to design an ASCII votesheet for issue nine of the Amiga (and Android) diskmag Versus produced by Nukleus and Void. Our entry was remotely submitted to the competition at the Edison 2019 demoparty held in Stockholm, Sweden. (We should have been there!)

Saturday 22nd June 2019

FuZioN releases an ASCII at the NOVA 19 demoparty

In an unprovoked burst of activity, FuZioN puts the rest of us to shame by actually creating a production and releasing it at a demoparty.

Our initial "born-ascii-tro" (!!) was released at the NOVA 19 party hosted in Budleigh Salterton in Devon on the south cost of England.

About

Basically we are a small splinter group of doods who were ex-AMIGA sceners and we are all based in the UK.

We still have an interest in both the retro computer scene and the demoscene, and we try to stay creative. It's the productive bit that's the hard part!
